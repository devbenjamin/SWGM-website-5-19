About Southwest Glass & Mirror

Southwest Glass & Mirror has been helping the people of our community since the '80s (1989), and we are stronger than ever. Thanks almost exclusively to word-of-mouth recommendations from previous clients we have helped, we have grown and thrived. 

Southwest Glass & Mirror was started with the knowledge that custom glass and mirror cutting and glazing always needs the utmost of care for our clients and a respect for their vision. With a discerning eye for detail, decades of experience and a passion for the satisfaction of having a job done well, we have earned our excellent reputation in the field.

Mike Woolley assumed command and ownership of Southwest Glass & Mirror in 2015. Since then, he has greatly grown our reputation and client base and has made Southwest Glass & Mirror the choice for discerning citizens and contractors, alike.  

Mike Woolley

Owner, Operator and Lead Glazier for Southwest Glass & Mirror



Learning the craft at a young age from his father and grandgather, Mike's approach to his work is simple; do the work as if his grandfather is watching over his shoulder, and make him proud.

Recently, Mike donated 450 custom cut mirrors for all the 3rd grade classes of the Collier County Public School system to aid in their lessons in the use of lasers.

Mike was consulted by local news teams regarding hurricane rediness and what we can do to help protect our homes and our families.

We are committed to the good of our community, the good of our clients and the good of our employees and their families.
